About the Artist

Sasha Brahman is a New York–based artist working primarily with oil and acrylic on canvas. Her paintings explore memory, spirituality, transformation, and the fragile relationship between light and darkness.

Selected Works

Fallen Angel

16 × 20 in · Oil & Acrylic

A haunting depiction of grace in collapse. “Fallen Angel” explores the beauty that remains even after innocence is lost.

Revelation

16 × 20 in

Inspired by the Book of Revelation, reflecting divine mystery and celestial power.
